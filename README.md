# KidShield – Child Safety App

**KidShield** is a simple and effective child safety app designed to help children stay safe and allow parents to quickly reach them in case of emergencies. The app provides direct call buttons for parents, ensuring that children can get help with just a single tap.

## Features
- **Direct Call Buttons** – Quickly call Mother or Father with one tap.
- **Simple & Clean UI** – Square or rounded buttons with clear labels.
- **Easy to Use** – Minimal setup; no complicated features required.
- **Optional Status Label** – Displays “Calling…” messages (can be hidden).

## How It Works
1. Open the app.
2. Tap **Call Mother** or **Call Father**.
3. The app directly initiates a phone call to the respective parent.
4. Optional: The label shows a “Calling…” message.

## UI Design
- **Buttons**: Square or rounded, filled with bright colors
- **Text**: Large and readable (20–24 pt), contrasting color
- **Optional Icons**: Phone icon on buttons for better clarity

## Future Improvements
- Add **SOS button** for emergencies
- Add **real-time location sharing** for parents
- Add **TinyDB support** to store parent numbers dynamically

## How to Use
1. Download and open the app on your Android device.
2. Tap the **Call Mother** or **Call Father** button.
3. The call will start automatically.
